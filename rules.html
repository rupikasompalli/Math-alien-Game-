Game Rules


  1. The aim of the game is to use the cannon to “shoot down” or destroy as many invading spaceships with correct answers.

  2. This must be done without any of the invading spaceships touching the cannon and/or the plank the cannon is on.


  3. You must use the left and the right arrow key to move the cannon. Once the cannon is moved or positioned underneath a spaceship then you must use the spacebar key to fire at the spaceship.


  4. The player will automatically lose the game if the cannon or plank is touched by an invading spaceship.


  5. The player has a ablity to play game within 90 seconds without touching cannon will win the game!!


  6. If the spaceships touch the cannon or plank.The player will losse the game!!
